About Me


Background

Christopher Newberry is studying to become a software engineer at the Florida Institute Of Technology. He is currently a software developer with a main focus on web-design. He started a company last year and his goal is to expand honest businesses by building custom websites.

Work

In 2019, Christopher volunteered at a website development company. For two years he learned the ins and outs of the website development industry. Last year Christopher started his own company, Newberry Software, to provide custom websites to businesses. Through his 3 years of website development, Christopher has helped several companies expand their audience and boost their client acquisition. Christopher will continue to grow Newberry Software and offer development positions to passionate software engineers and computer scientists. Christopher excels in several high-level languages including C, C++, C#, Python, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. He embraces new challenges, especially outside of his comfort zone. Christopher's interests outside of software development include baseball, guitar, and fishing.
