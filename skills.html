Marketable Skills and Abilities


Web-Design

I currently have 4 years of website design and development experience. I own and operate a website development company named Newberry Software that produces custom websites for any size business or client. I have worked with several langugages such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript as well as many different frameworks such as Django and Angular.

Corporate Management

I have held the responsibility of leading, directing, and administrating two software development companies (one of which I currently own). Corporate management, in my understanding, is more talent than skill. Great management is instinctual. I do not believe leadership can be properly taught. Corporate management requires strategic plans and efficient use of resources. The ability to effectively communicate with a diverse group of people and accomplish a common goal is the most important attribute of every leader.

Serial Innovation

My first major addition to a system was when I was in elementary school and revolutionized a recycling program to significantly reduce waste in the Belmont-Redwood Shores school district. Since then I have brought innovation to many places including sports training facilities, little league umpiring organizations, marketing companies, private investment groups, and my own business.

Client Prospecting

My father is an expert in client prospecting and spent years teaching me ways to find and approach new clients. Prospecting is nearly an art form that I have grown to appreciate and respect. Great prospecting saves a business valuable time and brings the best clients to the table.
